PRIVACY POLICY — MY CYCLE MANIA
Last updated: June 2026
Developer: KC3SMW

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SHORT VERSION
My Cycle Mania collects no personal data. Everything stays on your phone.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FULL POLICY

1. DATA WE COLLECT
   My Cycle Mania collects NO personal information whatsoever.
   We do not collect your name, email address, location history,
   device identifiers, or any other personal data.

2. GPS / LOCATION
   The app uses your device's GPS to display your position on the map
   and to record your rides. This data is processed entirely on your
   device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or
   external service.

   Ride tracks and GPS recordings are saved only to your phone's local
   storage (in the app's private files folder) and are never uploaded
   anywhere.

3. INTERNET ACCESS
   The app requests INTERNET permission solely to allow you to download
   OpenStreetMap tile files and routing data (OSM PBF files) during the
   one-time offline setup. Once those files are downloaded, the app
   operates entirely offline. No background data is ever sent or received.

4. IN-APP PURCHASES
   The optional "Buy Me a Coffee" tip is processed entirely through
   Google Play Billing. KC3SMW does not receive or store any payment
   information. All transaction handling is managed by Google.
   See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. CRASH REPORTING / ANALYTICS
   The app contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, advertising
   networks, or tracking of any kind.

6. THIRD-PARTY LIBRARIES
   The app uses the following open-source libraries, all of which
   operate entirely on-device:
   - OSMDroid (OpenStreetMap tile rendering)
   - GraphHopper (offline bicycle routing)
   - Google Play Services Location (FusedLocationProvider — on-device GPS)

7. DATA RETENTION
   Since no data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete.
   Ride files stored on your device can be deleted at any time by
   uninstalling the app or clearing app data in Android settings.

8. CHILDREN
   The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including
   children under 13.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
   If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the
   URL listed in the Play Store listing. The "Last updated" date above
   will reflect any changes.

10. CONTACT
    Questions about this privacy policy:
    GitHub: https://github.com/chengmania/MyCycleMania/issues

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This policy applies to the Android app "My Cycle Mania"
