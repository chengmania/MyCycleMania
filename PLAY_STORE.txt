FULL DESCRIPTION  (paste into "Full description")

My Cycle Mania is a passion project built by a cyclist, for cyclists. It's completely free — no ads, no accounts, no subscription, no data plan required once you're set up.

The goal was simple: build a fully offline cycling app that actually works when you're deep in the countryside with no signal.

━━ FEATURES ━━

🗺  OFFLINE MAPS
Powered by OpenStreetMap. Download your region once over Wi-Fi and your maps work forever — no internet needed on the road.

🚴  OFFLINE TURN-BY-TURN NAVIGATION
Bicycle routing powered by GraphHopper. Voice guidance announces street names, distances, and turn directions. Routes are calculated on your device with zero data.

📍  WAYPOINT ROUTE PLANNING
Long-press anywhere on the map to drop waypoints. The app instantly calculates your route, shows estimated distance and time, and draws the line on screen. Drag waypoints to adjust.

📡  GPS RIDE RECORDING
Records your full track in the background even with the screen off. Live stats show distance, duration, and current speed while you ride.

⛰  ELEVATION TRACKING
Tracks elevation gain throughout your ride.

📄  GPX EXPORT
Every completed ride is automatically saved as a GPX file you can import into Strava, Komoot, Garmin Connect, or any other platform.

🏁  RIDE SUMMARY
At the end of each ride you get a full summary: total distance, duration, average speed, max speed, and elevation gain — with a map thumbnail and the option to save a screenshot to your gallery.

🗂  CYCLE MAP OVERLAY
Switch between standard OSM and CyclOSM — a map layer designed specifically for cyclists showing bike lanes, trails, and terrain.

🏔  TERRAIN OVERLAY
Toggle an OpenTopoMap layer to see elevation contours while planning.

☕  SUPPORT THE PROJECT
Like the app? Buy the developer a coffee through Google Play — totally optional, always appreciated.

━━ FIRST-TIME SETUP ━━

My Cycle Mania is fully offline, which means you need to download two things before your first ride:

1. MAP TILES — The visual map for your region. For Eastern PA this is roughly 30 MB. Tap the menu → Download Map Tiles to get started.

2. ROUTING DATA — An OpenStreetMap PBF file for your state or region (Pennsylvania is ~120 MB). This is the road network GraphHopper uses to calculate your routes. Tap the menu → Download Routing Data to grab it.

After downloading, the app builds a routing index on your device the first time. This takes a few minutes — you'll see a notification saying "Building routing index." When it says "My Cycle Mania is ready!" you're all set and every route after that is instant.

Both downloads only need to happen once. You can do it at home on Wi-Fi and then ride all day with your phone in airplane mode.
